The Best Movies According to Kiran

My top 3 movies of all-time

Veera Kannadiga

Devastated by his father's mysterious death, a young man vows to safeguard his colony from a local criminal mastermind. Eventually, he embarks on a quest to avenge his father's murder

Appu

A young man ends up in the hospital in critical condition following a fight with a rival gang. Soon, he falls in love with a woman who donates blood to him for his treatment.

James

The life of Santosh changes after Vijay, owner of a criminal organisation called Gayakwad Syndicate, enlists his help in his business dealings.
